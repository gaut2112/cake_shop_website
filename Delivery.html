With the exception of wedding cake purchases, we must receive payment of the whole of the price for the goods that you order before your order can be accepted. For wedding cakes we ask for either a 50% deposit or payment in full. Where a wedding cake deposit has been paid the balance is due 8 days before the date the cake is collected or delivered. Once payment has been received by us we will confirm that your order has been accepted by sending an email to you at the email address you provide in your order form. Our acceptance of your order brings into existence a legally binding contract between us.

Other celebration cakes have to be ordered min a day before.Home delivery can be done but incurrs an extra charge according to the distance.

Special orders are excepted and the customers get a concession on the special days like the anniversaries and birthdays as per registered in the account
